Subsistence Farming

Subsistence farming is a type of agriculture where farmers grow crops and raise animals primarily for their own consumption. It involves the use of traditional tools, small plots of land, and limited or no use of modern technology.

This method is common in rural areas and is focused on food security rather than profit.
