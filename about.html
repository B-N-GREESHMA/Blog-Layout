About Farming World

Farming World is a simple educational website designed to provide basic knowledge about different types of farming practices around the world.

The website presents key farming methods such as subsistence, commercial, organic, and mixed farming. It is intended for students, beginners, and anyone interested in agriculture to gain a clear understanding of these topics in a visually engaging and beginner-friendly manner.

This is a non-commercial project built for learning purposes. All information is summarized for easy reading and educational use.

We hope this platform helps spark curiosity and build awareness about how food is grown, what methods farmers use, and how farming impacts the environment.
